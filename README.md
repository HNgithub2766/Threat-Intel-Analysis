# Threat-Intel-Analysis

## Overview

This repository contains a threat research report, adversary profiles, and cyber threat intelligence assessments developed as part of my Telstrs TRI internship in 2023. 

The reports focus on analysing threat actors, ransomware groups, advanced persistent threats (APTs), attack techniques, and emerging cyber threats affecting organisations across various industries. Research is conducted using publicly available intelligence sources and mapped to industry frameworks such as MITRE ATT&CK.

## Objectives

* Develop practical threat intelligence and threat research skills.
* Analyse cyber threat actors and their tactics, techniques and procedures (TTPs).
* Produce structured intelligence reports suitable for security operations and risk management teams.
* Map adversary behaviours to the MITRE ATT&CK framework.
* Improve understanding of current cyber threats impacting organisations.
